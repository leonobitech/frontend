## TL;DR

Claude is Anthropic's AI assistant — built on **Constitutional AI** to be helpful, harmless, and honest. Unlike other AI tools, Claude is designed as a **thinking partner** you can steer: shape its personality, tone, and behavior to match how you work.

**Key highlights from this episode:**

- **Constitutional AI** — Claude is trained on values, not just data. It can say "I'm not sure" instead of hallucinating.
- **Hybrid models** — Opus and Sonnet offer instant answers or extended thinking for complex reasoning.
- **1 million token context** — Claude Opus 4.6 processes ~2,500 pages in a single conversation.
- **Available everywhere** — Claude.ai, Claude Code, Slack, and Excel.
