## TL;DR

The best way to talk to Claude is like you'd brief a smart colleague who just joined your team. Structure your prompts with three layers: **Setting the Stage** (your role and context), **Defining the Task** (what you need), and **Specifying Rules** (format, tone, constraints).

**Key highlights from this episode:**

- Talk to Claude naturally — like a coworker, not a search engine.
- Structure prompts in 3 parts: context, task, rules.
- Upload files directly — Claude analyzes PDFs, spreadsheets, code, and images.
- Iterate through dialogue — follow-ups, feedback, and redirects refine results.
