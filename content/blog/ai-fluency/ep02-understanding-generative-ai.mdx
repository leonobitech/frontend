## TL;DR

Generative AI creates new content rather than just analyzing data. It was made possible by three pillars: **Transformer algorithms** (2017), the **explosion of digital data**, and massive **computing power** (GPUs).

**Key highlights from this episode:**

- **Traditional vs Generative AI** — classification vs creation, a fundamental shift
- **The training pipeline** — Pre-training (pattern learning), Fine-tuning (human feedback alignment), Deployment (prompt-response)
- **Key capabilities** — versatile language skills, general purpose abilities, pattern learning, tool connections
- **Current limitations** — knowledge cutoff, hallucinations, context limits, reasoning challenges
- **Knowing limitations = Discernment** — one of the 4D Framework competencies from Episode 01
