## TL;DR

AI Fluency is the ability to collaborate with AI in ways that are **effective, efficient, ethical, and safe**. It's built on the **4D Framework** — four interconnected competencies that stay relevant no matter what AI system you use.

**Key highlights from this episode:**

- **AI skills expire** — if they're tied to a specific tool, they won't last through the next update
- **AI Fluency** is a framework of skills, knowledge, and values that transcends any single AI system
- **The 4Ds**: Delegation (when to involve AI), Description (how to communicate with AI), Discernment (how to evaluate AI output), Diligence (how to use AI responsibly)
- **Three modes of interaction**: Automation (AI executes), Augmentation (AI collaborates), Agency (AI acts independently)
- **These skills compound** — every interaction makes you better at the next one

Based on the [AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations](https://courses.anthropic.com/) course from Anthropic Academy.
