## TL;DR

Six foundational prompting techniques that work with any AI system, each with practical before/after examples showing the difference good communication makes.

**Key highlights from this episode:**

- **Provide context** — be specific about scope, geography, timeframe
- **Show examples** — demonstrating a pattern is more powerful than describing it
- **Specify constraints** — define format, length, structure, and style up front
- **Break into steps** — guide the AI's reasoning process explicitly
- **Ask it to think first** — giving space to reason leads to better answers
- **Define the AI's role** — role, tone, and style shape the entire response
- **Secret weapon** — ask the AI to help you write a better prompt
- **Prompting is iterative** — Prompt, Response, Refine, repeat
