## TL;DR

The 4D Framework provides four interconnected competencies for thoughtful AI collaboration: **Delegation**, **Description**, **Discernment**, and **Diligence**. Each has three sub-components, and together they form a continuous cycle that improves with every interaction.

**Key highlights from this episode:**

- **Delegation** — Problem Awareness, Platform Awareness, Task Delegation: know your goals, know AI's capabilities, divide the work
- **Description** — Product, Process, Performance: tell AI what you want, how to approach it, and how to behave
- **Discernment** — Evaluate the output, the reasoning, and the interaction style. Don't accept the first draft
- **Diligence** — Choose AI thoughtfully, be transparent, take responsibility for everything you share
- **One example, four Ds** — a blog post about remote work trends shows how all four competencies work together in practice
- **The Diligence Statement** — a simple template for transparency when sharing AI-assisted work
